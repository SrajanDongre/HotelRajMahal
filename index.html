Hotel Raj Mahal

The Raj Mahal Hotel is the Palace of the King of Jaipur Maharana JaiChand Bhawani Singh Rajput built in 1822 for the Royal Family of Jaipur which is now redeveloped as Hotel and managed by the Raj Mahal Group of Hotels . The Hotel is still carring the legecy of the Maharana by preserving the heritage and serving the hospitality to our guests .

The ultimate goal is not not just for our guests to become loyal to our brand but become advocates for our business , incentivising other travellers to stay with us and experience the hospitality . Kindness and courtesy as a foundation of our service goes a long way in making sure hotel always leave our guests feeling satisfied and happy .
